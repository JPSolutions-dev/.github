## 👋 About

Hi, I'm the developer behind **JP Solutions** — a small, independent software and web
development studio based in Germany. I build modern, reliable digital tools for
**small businesses, freelancers, and clubs (Vereine)** — the kind of clients who often
find full-service agencies too expensive or too impersonal.

No account managers, no overhead, no jargon. Just a direct line to the person who
actually writes your code, and clean solutions built around your budget and your needs.

## 🛠️ What I Do

| Service | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🌐 **Websites & PWAs** | Fast, responsive websites — optionally as installable Progressive Web Apps. |
| 🧩 **Custom Web Applications** | Login systems, membership management, booking tools, and more. |
| 🗄️ **Database Design** | Clean, well-structured data architecture with PostgreSQL & co. |
| 🔧 **Maintenance & Support** | Updates, bug fixes, and dependable technical support. |
| 💡 **Digitalization Consulting** | Turning manual club or business workflows into digital processes. |
